## Privacy Policy

### 1. Introduction 
Welcome to DueDate. Your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how DueDate ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information. Unlike most financial apps, DueDate is built with a Privacy-First philosophy: the app is fully functional offline and does not use any cloud servers to store your financial data.

### 2. Data Collection and Usage
- Local Financial Data: All bill information, bank names, card details, and SMS parsing results are stored locally in a secure database on your physical device. We have no access to this data.
- SMS Permissions: The app requests permission to read SMS messages solely to detect and extract bill information automatically. This process happens entirely on your device. No SMS content is ever transmitted to us or any third party.
- Personal Information: We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your name to use the app.

### 3. User-Initiated Communication 
The only instance where we receive any information from you is if you choose to contact us manually (e.g., via the "Report Issue" or "Feedback" buttons).
- Feedback/Reports: If you send a "Bill Parser Report," the app will pre-fill an email with the specific SMS text and parsing result you selected. You have the opportunity to review and edit this text before sending it.
- Email Address: When you email us at mateyouapps@gmail.com or mateyou@tuta.io, we will see your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We use this information only to respond to your request or to improve our bill parsing patterns.

### 4. Data Security 
Since your data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's security. We recommend using built-in Android security features (like screen locks and biometrics) to prevent unauthorized access to your device and the DueDate app.

### 5. Third-Party Services 
DueDate does not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) or advertising networks. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

### 6. Changes to This Policy 
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's settings or through an app update. Your continued use of the app after changes are made signifies your acceptance of the new policy.

## Terms of Use

- **Personal Use**: This app is intended for personal tracking only.
- **No Warranty**: DueDate is provided "as is." While we strive for 100% parsing accuracy, we are not responsible for missed bills, incorrect due dates, or any financial penalties (late fees) resulting from the use of this app. Always verify your bill details with your bank.
- **Offline Responsibility**: Since the app is offline, you are responsible for backing up your device. If you lose your phone or inadvertently clear app data, your stored bills will be lost.
